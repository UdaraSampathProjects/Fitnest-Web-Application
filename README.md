#### Brief Description of your Solution - 
Product Name- FitNest
The objective of our project is to design and develop a specialized social media platform aimed at catering to the needs of fitness enthusiasts, providing them with a dedicated space to share their fitnes, progress, workout routines, and valuable healthy lifestyle advice. Our platform’s name is “FitNest”. Central to our endeavor is the construction of a robust REST API infrastructure, leveraging the Java Spring Boot framework, coupled with the development of a client-facing web application built on React. Through this web application, users will have the capability to share multimedia content such as pictures and videos, enabling them to showcase their fitness achievements and experiences effectively. Additionally, users will have the functionality to update their current workout status, allowing for real-time tracking of their fitness journey. Furthermore, the platform will facilitate the sharing of comprehensive workout plans and meal plans, fostering a supportive and informative community environment conducive to achieving fitness goals.

Main functions related to our web application are,
User management,Posting pictures and videos,Updating current workout status ,Workout plan sharing,Meal plan sharingand Handling notifications.
